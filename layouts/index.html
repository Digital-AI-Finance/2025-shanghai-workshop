October 20-23, 2025  |  Shanghai, China

About the Conference

The 23rd China International Talent Exchange Conference (CIEP 2025) brings together international scholars, leading journal editors, and emerging researchers to discuss cutting-edge advances in data science, fintech, and the digital economy. This prestigious gathering features roundtable discussions, editorial meetings, and a young scholars forum, fostering collaboration between Chinese and international academic communities.

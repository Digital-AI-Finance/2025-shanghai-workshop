# 23rd China International Talent Exchange Conference

## Data Science, Fintech and Digital Economy

**October 20-23, 2025 | Shanghai, China**

---

## Event Sessions

### Roundtable: Economic Resilience in Emerging Markets
**October 20, 2025 | Shanghai Yoja Hotel**

An in-depth discussion among international scholars on economic resilience in emerging markets.

**Participants:**
- Susan Sharma (Deakin University)
- Liu Guanyu (Capital University of Economics and Business)
- Georg Keilbar (Humboldt University of Berlin)
- Joerg Osterrieder (University of Twente)
- Lu Qiao (Leeds University)
- Suyang Li (Swansea University)

---

### A China's Story, A Global Research: Meeting with Editors
**October 22, 2025 | Shanghai World Expo Conference Center**

Promoting research achievements in philosophy and social sciences with Chinese characteristics; disseminating outstanding Chinese research internationally.

**Journal Editors Present:**
- Jianqing Fan (Journal of the American Statistical Association)
- Wolfgang Haerdle (Digital Finance)
- Jonathan Batten (Finance Research Letters)
- Peter Szilagyi (Journal of International Financial Markets, Institutions & Money)
- Joerg Osterrieder (Management and Marketing)

---

### Towards The Future: Young Scholars Forum
**October 22, 2025 | Shanghai World Expo Conference Center**

**Keynote Speaker:** Joerg Osterrieder

**Topic:** "Digital Finance: Non-Systematic Systematics, LLM Narratives, and High-Frequency Trading Around Macro-News Announcements"

**Organizer:** Capital University of Economics and Business (CUEB)
